About RCBA

The Rotaract Club of Bombay Airport is a community based non-profit organization under the Rotaract District 3141. We, a group of agile youngsters nobly believe in our motto "Act For Impact" & passionately thrive towards achieving it; by uplifting society through our social and community service, development of one and all as well as recreation. ​

My responsibilites

As a Department Head for both International Service and Professional Development, I managed diverse responsibilities that enhanced both the global reach and professional growth of our club members. Leading the Professional Development department with a team of 10 members, I orchestrated various skill-building events including LinkedIn optimization workshops and resume development sessions, focused on elevating our members' professional profiles. Simultaneously, in my role heading the International Service department with another team of 10 members, I facilitated cross-cultural collaborations by organizing joint events with clubs worldwide. This dual leadership role strengthened my abilities in team management, event planning, and international communication while developing programs that delivered tangible value to our members' professional growth and global networking opportunities.
